Deleting Artifacts

What it is

Artifacts may be deleted from the Artifact Explorer.
Caution should be taken in performing this task because along with the artifact all of its default hierarchy children will also be deleted.

How to do it

To delete an artifact and its children select the desired artifact and the right click, next select the “Delete Artifact” menu item. A dialog will be displayed asking for deletion confirmation.


Note:

An artifact may also be deleted by selecting an artifact and pressing the delete key.
